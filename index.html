Tad-Hack Global Hackathon 2020

Participant-Team lead
Project : iSikweletu Financial Systems

The Tad-Hack Global Hackathon is a world wide event that happens anually, during the year 2020 I was the leader of a team made of myself and fellow critical thinker Wandile Mnyandu to build a financial system that is aimed at uniting small loan seekers and those that provide the service, while MTN was one of the sponsors the MOMO API was our choice Application programming Interface for cash distribution by the users in the system.

It's a web based system with the technologies that were used being HTML,CSS,Javascript,Bootstrap,MySQL,PHP.
I contributed towards the development of the system by creating the logo, designing and coding the Landing Page, Register,Login, Incoming Transactions Page, Outgoing Transactions page and the Loan Management page in the front end, while I also contributed to the designing and development of the database of the system.
